Orbit Prediction(OP) & Orbit Tracking Control System (OTCS) - Digi Cube

Introduction

As part of my ongoing MEng project, Designing a Generic Digital Twin for a CubeSat, I have been tasked with the section Orbit Prediction (OP) & Orbit Tracking Control System(OTCS). I have been developing a MATLAB-based simulation to model and predict the orbital dynamics of a Cubesat in Low Earth Orbit (LEO). My work has focused on accurately propagating the CubeSat's trajectory and determining the necessary adjustments for orbital manoeuvres. This simulation marks a key milestone in the project, enabling real-time analysis of orbital behaviour. Moving forward, I aim to refine the model by incorporating external perturbations and enhancing its predictive accuracy based on the mission's requirements, whilst also expanding the propagation of the CubeSat by modelling a rendezvous with an asteroid.
